About Me

Hello! My name is Illia Tarabarov. I’m 18 years old and I study at Polytechnic High School in ceske budejovice (SSPCB) in the Czech Republic. Right now, I live in the beautiful city of České Budějovice, where I enjoy both my studies and the lovely nature around me.
I speak Russian and Ukrainian fluently because they are my native languages. I am also studying Czech and English, which allows me to comfortably communicate and work in a multilingual environment. These skills let me explore new things and adjust to different situations easily.
My hobbies and interests are important role in my life and shape my personality.
I love music because it helps me relax and feel balanced. I’m also like cycling, which keeps me fit and allows me enjoy the outdoors. Studying is a big part of my life, and I’m always looking to learn and make my dreams a reality.
I see myself as a friendly and adaptable person. These traits help me handle various situations well. I also enjoy traveling, which gives me new experiences and inspiration.
Living in České Budějovice makes me feel positive and energetic. This city is not just where I study but also where I can develop as a person and find new opportunities
